6. Support inclusions and alterations with percentages. Need some type of markup for this. A sentence or phrase  can
    be marked as randomly included based on a percentage.
    Examples
    %%.3:{You see a door to the south.} could mean "include this here  30% of  the time."
    %%{The air is cold.|The frigid air bites} could mean chose one or the other with equal proability (50-50 here)
    %%{.3::The air is cold.|The frigid air bites} "choose first alternate 30% of the time otherwise use second"
    %%{.1::Foo|.1::Bar|Baz} "choose Foo 10% of the time, Bar 10%, or Baz 80%"
     note: the tool  would facilitate entering the markup. The game engine would need to interpret the mark up to
        determine what text to display to the user.
